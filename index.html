The architecture

Your life is made of four pillars.

Most people optimize one. The extraordinary design all four. Your health, your relationships, your money, and your work belong on the same surface — because in your actual life, they already are.

Health

Training, sleep, recovery, breath. HealthKit-deep — steps, heart rate, workouts to the rep — with a Healthspan Score computed from your live data.

The deep pillar

Relationships

The Sunday call to your parents. The weekly real conversation. Intentions about the people you love — scheduled and kept, not just meant.

Finance

Career

“Ship the side project.” “Make senior by Q3.” The cascade turns ambition into deep-work blocks placed around your real week.

How it works

Goal in. Scheduled action out.

Every pillar runs on the same spine. State a goal in any of the four, and Spark carries it from vision to a concrete action on your real calendar — then tracks whether it happened.

  1. State the goal

    In your own words, in any pillar. No forms, no taxonomy to learn.

    “Save $10K by December.”
    “Run a half marathon.”
    “Call my parents every Sunday.”

  2. Spark cascades it

    Yearly → quarterly → monthly → weekly. Set the vision once; the system breaks it down.

    Yearly vision
    ↳ Quarterly milestones
      ↳ Monthly targets
        ↳ Weekly actions

  3. It lands on your calendar

    At least one concrete weekly action, written into your real week — around what’s already there.

    create_event →
    Tue 6:45 AM · Easy 5K
    Sun 9:00 AM · Budget review

  4. Completion is tracked

    Progress rolls back up the hierarchy. When life shifts, the cascade adapts — no starting over.

    Week 3 · 2 of 3 done
    Q3 milestone · 64%
    replanning Thursday…

Every “AI coach” before this one talked. Spark’s unit of work is a scheduled action, not a paragraph of advice.

The deep pillar

Health runs deepest.

Every pillar gets the engine. Health also gets a full data vertical — because your body generates the richest signal you own, and Spark should be reading it.

  • HealthKit, all the way down

    Steps, heart rate, sleep, workouts — per set, per rep, per weight. A working integration, not a checkbox.

  • Workout screenshot OCR

    Snap the gym screen or a workout log. Spark extracts the exercises, sets, and weights into a real session — no retyping.

  • Breathwork, built in

    Guided sessions you can start from a Spark suggestion — a downshift between the deep-work block and dinner.

  • Healthspan Score

    One number computed from your live HealthKit metrics — and it moves when the underlying data does.

Honest scope: Relationships, Finance, and Career run on the cascade engine — goal, plan, calendar, tracked. Health carries the deep data vertical first. The others deepen from there.

The other half of the spec

What SparQan refuses.

A product is defined as much by what it won’t do. These are commitments, not caveats.

Generic advice

Spark reads your goals, calendar, and health before it speaks. Advice that could apply to anyone is a defect, not a feature.

Ads, ever

No ads. No third-party data brokers. No ad SDKs in the bundle. Your coaching conversations are not inventory.

Hustle aesthetics

No flames, no leaderboards, no streaks-as-pressure. Streaks exist as memory, not as guilt.

Subscription-only pricing

Free to start. Premium when you’re ready — including a lifetime tier, planned, for people who want off the monthly treadmill.

Single-pillar tunnel vision

There will never be a “fitness-only mode.” The four pillars are the architecture, not a theme.

Pretending to be therapy

SparQan is a life-design tool, not clinical care. If you’re in crisis, it points you to real resources — and gets out of the way.

These aren’t v1 limitations. These are the product.
